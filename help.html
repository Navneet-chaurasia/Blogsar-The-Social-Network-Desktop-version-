How to use "Blogsar", working of all the icons are given below :-

  • First of all you have to complete sign up process. If you are already logged in then you can skip this step.

  • This is the home button where you can see articles based on your followed topics.

  • By clicking this icon you can see your personal details also you can edit your personal information.

  • By clicking this icon the article editor will be open. Here you can write your articles and you can add description after that you can post your article on "Blogsar".

  • This is the search button where you can search the articles, the authors and channels.

  • Channel Name:- Create your best channel name because it will be visible on the top of your article.

  • Author Name:- Author name will be visible on your every article as a writer.

  • Author Strength:-Author strength is a term that describe popularity of author .It will be claculated by your number of subscribers,total article views,total article likes,total article feedbacks and total number of articles.

  • Article Strength:-Article strength is a term that describe popularity of articles It will be calculated by number of views, likes and feedback of that perticuler article.

  • How to Block asking message:-If any one ask you bad question and you do not want that he/she send you any further message then you can go to discussion, at top right corner of that message there is a three bar menu where block option is listed. By clicking block he/she will not be able to ask you further messages.

  • How to Block feedbacks:-If any one sends you inaproprite comments as a feedback in your articles and you want to block that person for sending further feedbacks. Go to that particular feedback or any feedback of that author in discussion page. At top right corner there is block feedbacks option listed.Click that option and he/she will not able to send you further feedbacks on your articles.

  • How to Delete Article:-Go to personal wall page and click top right three dot button on that perticuler post and delete.

  • How to Report:-If you found anything bad and you want to report then click top right three dot button and report to "Blogsar". After you report that article will immediately be disappera from your home feed. Article will be reviewed by our team and if any thing found suspicious than article will be removed. You can see action taken on you reported articles in report history page.

  • What is snoozing:-In each article, there is a facility called snoozing feedbacks. If you snooze feedbacks than feedback giving facility will be disabled in that article . And no one will be able to give feedback on that article.

  • How to reset forgotten password:-If you have forgotten your password, than logout and click forgotten password in login page. Where you can reset your password through your registerd email id.
